With a view to Propagate 1. The Avatara Tatva of Sri Bhagavanji and 2. The Sacred Message of Viwaguru as envisaged in the great epic of Srimad Bhagavadbharati, in the entire World Srimad Bhagavadbharati Trust was formed on 28-01-1983 with its location at Sri Viswaguru Vidyaranya Bhagavan Math itself , with the following objectives:

to protect and propagate the Vedic Culture and Tradition among all human beings.

to train and develop Pracaraks and Adhyayana centres world wide for the purpose as envisaged in ( 1) & ( 2) above.
to help poor in respect of Education and Medical aid
to develop Unity and Fraternity among people by cultivating Sarvatma Bhavana for achievement of Vasudhika Kutumbakam
to work in unison with other organizations having similar views as envisaged above
to secure both movable and immovable assets for developing and promoting the Trust Values with Selfless Dedicated Services
